READ BELOW:

Each files in this directory is loaded throught EMF.
Please do not make your entity spawnable via spawnmenu.
Make sure to register your ent in EMF with EMF.AddEnt( 'classofyourent' ) at the end of your ent script.
Each entity created in this directory will have a chance to be spawned on the map.
Do not make entities using very big models it will be spawned out of the map in 80% of cases.
